# WireGuard Setup – Android

## Step 1: Install WireGuard
Open **Google Play Store** and install:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wireguard.android

## Step 2: Add a Tunnel
1. Open the WireGuard app
2. Tap **➕**
3. Choose **Scan from QR code**
4. Scan the QR code provided in the email

> Alternatively, select **Import from file** and choose the `.conf` file.

## Step 3: Connect
Toggle the tunnel **ON**.
