## Step 1: Install WireGuard
Download from the **App Store**:
https://apps.apple.com/app/id1441195209

## Step 2: Import Configuration
1. Open the WireGuard app
2. Tap **Add a Tunnel**
3. Select **Create from QR Code**
4. Scan the QR code sent to your email

> You may also choose **Import from File** and select the `.conf` file.

## Step 3: Enable VPN
Toggle the tunnel **ON** and allow VPN permissions.
